## NOTES

### Notation

The key combination `Ctrl+V` may be referred to as `^V`, `Ctrl-V` or `<C-V>`.

### VIM MODES

- Normal (standard, press `esc` to return to it)

- Insert `i`

- Replace `r`

- Selection modes (visual `v`)
  - line `<S-V>`
  - block `<C-V>`

- Command line `:`
